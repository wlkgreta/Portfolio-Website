Whitney Greta

Front-End Developer

I'm a front-end developer based in Des Moines, IA.

My interest in coding actually began when I was working as an Account & Brand manager for a local brewery and helped consult on their new website design. I was hooked!

So in 2024 I finally took the plunge and started taking classes online. In early 2025, I earned my Front-End Engineer certificate and, since then, have been working on projects to continue learning and honing my skills. Professionally, I have ten years of experience working in customer-facing roles, with a majority of that being in sales and brand management. With that, I understand the importance of effective communication and attention to detail when it comes to creating exceptional client experiences.

In my free time, I enjoy doing puzzles with my husband while watching Survivor, playing video games, biking to breweries, and hanging out with our two cats.
