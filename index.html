Whitney Greta

Front-End Developer

I'm a front-end developer based in Des Moines, IA.

I have previously worked in the craft beer industry for 10 years. My interest in coding began when I was working as an Account & Brand manager for a local brewery and helped consult on their new website design. I was hooked.

So at the start of 2024 I finally took the plunge and started taking classes on Codecademy. In early 2025, I earned my Front-End Engineer certificate and, since then, have been working on projects to continue learning and honing my skills. My experiences working in sales and brand management have also given me a unique understanding when it comes to meeting client needs, including effective communication skills and a strong attention to detail.

In my free time, I enjoy doing puzzles with my husband while watching Survivor, playing video games, biking to breweries, and hanging out with our two cats.
